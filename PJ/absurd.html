2024 An Absurd trip to Urbino


In this book, photographs taken by Jason Fulford in Giancarlo De Carlo's architectural works in Urbino are arranged to construct short narratives. Each chapter, corresponding to a sequence of images, builds its own story. The photos are curated to guide the reader through visual tales, allowing the architecture to be interpreted through these brief, self-contained narratives.
